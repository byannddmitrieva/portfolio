About

Strategy in mind.
Craft at heart.

Art Director and Brand Designer with international experience across Europe, Russia and the US.

My work centers on branding, communication design and typography. I build expressive, structured systems that remain recognizable across digital products, print, campaigns and physical spaces.

I’ve worked with Yandex, Samolet, Russian Post, Lamborghini, Forbes, PANGAIA, TEEKANNE and others — leading concepts, teams and production from the first idea to final delivery.
